Pizza Sauce

This sauce is perfect to pair with a great dough for pizza or even cheese sticks for

dipping sauce. It's an easy sauce to put together and one you will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon ground oregano
  • 1 Â½ teaspoons dried minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika

Steps

  1. Mix together tomato sauce and tomato paste in a medium bowl until smooth.
  2. Stir in oregano, garlic, and paprika to pizza sauce mixture.
  3. Enjoy!
